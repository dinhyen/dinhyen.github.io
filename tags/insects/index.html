Costa Rica Day 6 (Sunday) - Monteverde to San Jose

We went down to breakfast and ran into the Coopers. They had survived the trip from Arenal after all. However, this would be the last time that we saw them. First stop was the butterfly garden, also infamous as the residence of the tarantulas. Our guide, Alan, must have come straight out of Walter’s “crazy biologist guy” mold. Again extremely knowledgeable about his charges, he was full of information on the insects and arachnids that resided in the terrariums.
