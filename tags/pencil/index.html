Drawing hair with pencil

When drawing portrait with a pencil, I find it challenging to get realistic-looking hair. You may not want to draw individual strands of hair. If you’re not careful, the person may end up looking like Gollum. I follow an approach of first putting down a base layer by using a soft pencil to fill out the shape of the hair and blending stumps to create a uniform appearance. The blending stumps are used to effect a smooth, gradual transition between light and dark which is more realistic.
