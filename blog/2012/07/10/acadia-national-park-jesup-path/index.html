Acadia National Park: Jesup Path

Jul 10th, 2012

Map of our hike on Jesup Path.

Along with Emery Path, Jesup Path is another trail which can be found at the Sieur de Monts spring area. Straight and level, it’s hardly a challenging hike. Its claim to fame, I think, is an eternity of boardwalk amid a picturesque forest of birch trees and tall grass.
